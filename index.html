Help

Visualize pathfinding algorithms by selecting positions for the start node, finish node, and walls. To draw walls, select the 'wall' radio button in the options bar, then click and draw with your mouse. To undo a wall, hold down shift, click and draw with your mouse. To select which pathfinding algorithm to use, click on the dropdown menu in the options bar next to 'Algorithm'.
